Hackathon 2020

Hello, as Developer Student Clubs, we are here with ML Days event!

So what is ML Days? What will we learn?



The ML Days event is a training series created by bringing together educators specialized in Machine Learning. A great environment is waiting for you to find the answers to the most curious questions such as how we can progress in this field, how we can improve ourselves, and also to learn machine learning algorithms popular in our new educational series.


We aim to identify new ideas and projects aimed at protecting animals, preventing the violence and poor living conditions that animals are subjected to and improving their living conditions.
