What is ML Days Hackathon?

ML Days Hackathon is an event started to reinforce the machine learning algorithms learned during two and half months of training which is a continuation of ML Days events.

Hackathon aims to find solutions to the problems experienced by animals in line with our topic and to support them in their lives.
